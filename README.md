# NBA-Player-Impact
NBA Players who do all the little things in order to win

This will include: 
## Screen Impact Score:
Track how often a player's screens directly lead to an open shot or score.
Existing stats count screen assists, but you could include:
Hockey assists after screens.
Amount of separation created.
Expected points generated.

## Chaos Index
Measure how much disruption a player creates defensively:

Deflections.
Forced passes.
Shot-clock pressure.
Double teams that lead to turnovers.


## Clutch Gravity
Track how much defensive attention stars attract in late-game situations.

Number of defenders within a certain radius.
How often they force help defense.
Open shots created for teammates.

## Off-Ball Activity Rate
Distance moved while not possessing the ball combined with:

Cuts.
Relocations.
Screens set.
Speed changes.


## Momentum Swing Score
Assign values to events that energize a team:

Steals.
Offensive rebounds.
Blocks.
And-one plays.
